#############################
 Contributing to the Project
#############################

If you would like to participate in the jmbde project, you are welcome
to do so. I would be very happy about that. Please note the
<https://github.com/jmuelbert/AnniversaryReminder/blob/master/CONTRIBUTING.md>.

********************
 As a non-developer
********************

-  `Report issues
   <https://github.com/jmuelbert/AnniversaryReminder/issues>`_

-  Update our `Wiki
   <https://github.com/jmuelbert/AnniversaryReminder/wiki>`_ to make
   sure the information on it is up to date.

*************************
 As a software developer
*************************

-  You don't have to know C++. There are developers who got into jmbde
   development while learning C++ along the way.

-  Register an account on our development platform `Github
   <https://github.com/jmuelbert/AnniversaryReminder>`_ and fork our
   code.

-  Get familiar with the code. Pick a bug off of the `Issues List
   <https://github.com/jmuelbert/AnniversaryReminder/issues>`_

**Contributing to jmbde in 2 Easy Steps:**

#. Pick a bug off of the `Issues List
   <https://github.com/jmuelbert/AnniversaryReminder/issues>`_

#. Fix it! *Done!* Your name will now be on the `AnniversaryReminder
   contributor list
   <https://github.com/jmuelbert/AnniversaryReminder/blob/master/CONTRIBUTORS>`_.
